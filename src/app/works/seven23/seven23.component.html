Personal project Seven23

Seven23 is designed to answer the very simple question: 'How much did I spend on something this month'. The idea is not to track your portfolio, how much you saved or is left, but to understand where money goes and identify potential cognitive biases.

With privacy by design, the app provide secure end-to-end encryption within an open source code base to protect data confidentiality. This ensures that only its owner can access and read them.
